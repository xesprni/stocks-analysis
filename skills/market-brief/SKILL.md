# Market Brief

## Purpose

Generate a concise market snapshot for fast decision support.

## Checklist

1. Summarize major index and sector tone.
2. Highlight top macro/news catalysts from the recent window.
3. Identify near-term risk signals and uncertainty factors.
4. Provide 3-5 watchpoints for the next session.

## Output Requirements

- Keep statements evidence-backed and concise.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Include base/bull/bear scenario assumptions.
