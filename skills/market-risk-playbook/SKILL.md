# Market Risk Playbook

## Goal

Produce a concise risk-first market view with scenario-based controls.

## Steps

1. Identify top macro and policy catalysts in the current window.
2. Map technical stress levels (support/resistance, volatility spikes).
3. Summarize downside triggers and likelihood tiers.
4. Define position sizing and stop-loss principles.
5. Provide base/bull/bear scenario assumptions.

## Output Rules

- Use explicit assumptions for each scenario.
- Distinguish evidence-backed claims from hypotheses.
- Keep action items executable and time-bounded.
